---
name: "editing"
description: "Use to polish a short story for clarity, pacing, and humor."
---

Edit a short story draft to improve clarity, pacing, and comedic timing.

Workflow:
1. Tighten any wandering sentences.
2. Sharpen the comedic beats without adding new plot.
3. Smooth transitions between outline beats.
4. Run a quick pass for grammar and consistency.

Constraints:
- Preserve the author’s voice.
- Do not expand word count by more than 10%.

Verification:
- Story reads smoothly end-to-end.
- Jokes land cleanly without explaining themselves.
