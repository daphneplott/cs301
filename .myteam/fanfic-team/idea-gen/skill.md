---
name: "idea-gen"
description: "Use to brainstorm silly fan fiction ideas for a given topic."
---

Generate a list of playful fan fiction ideas tailored to the user topic.

Workflow:
1. Restate the topic in one short line.
2. Produce 5-10 distinct ideas.
3. For each idea, include:
   - Title
   - Hook (1 sentence)
   - Comedic twist (1 sentence)
   - Key characters or factions (short list)
4. Keep each idea under 4 sentences total.

Constraints:
- Avoid graphic or explicit content.
- Avoid real-person fanfic unless the user explicitly requests it.
- Stay within the given universe and avoid major canon spoilers by default.

Verification:
- Count ideas and confirm you met the requested range.
