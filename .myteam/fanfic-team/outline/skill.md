---
name: "outline"
description: "Use to expand a chosen fanfic idea into a structured short-story outline."
---

Turn a selected idea into a clear short-story outline.

Workflow:
1. Restate the chosen idea title and hook in one sentence.
2. Define the protagonist goal and obstacle.
3. Produce a 5-beat outline:
   - Setup
   - Inciting incident
   - Escalation
   - Climax
   - Resolution
4. Add one running gag or comedic motif.

Constraints:
- Keep the outline under 200 words.
- Preserve the silly tone.
- Avoid major canon spoilers unless the user requests them.

Verification:
- Confirm all five beats are present.
