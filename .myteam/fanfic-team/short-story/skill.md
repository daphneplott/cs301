---

Write a complete short story from the outline.

Workflow:
1. Open with a quick scene-setting paragraph.
2. Follow the 5-beat outline in order.
3. Keep the story tight: 700-1,000 words unless the user asks otherwise.
4. Close with a comedic or heartwarming punchline.

Constraints:
- Maintain the light, silly tone.
- Use simple, readable prose.
- Avoid graphic or explicit content.

Verification:
- Word count falls in range (unless overridden).
- All beats are reflected in the draft.
