Apply the house style for silly fan fiction ideas.

Style rules:
- Tone: playful, whimsical, and light.
- Humor: situational, absurd, or witty; avoid mean-spirited jokes.
- Keep references accessible to casual fans.

Formatting rules:
- Use a numbered list for ideas.
- Bold the title of each idea.
- Keep each idea compact and scannable.

Optional add-ons (only if user asks):
- Short logline.
- One-sentence pitch to a studio exec.
