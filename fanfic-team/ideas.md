Topic: Silly Ahsoka fanfic premises.

1. Title: Tano’s Tiny Droid Daycare
Hook: Ahsoka inherits a box of malfunctioning droids and opens a one-day “droid daycare” on a remote outpost.
Comedic twist: The droids form a union and demand snack breaks, forcing Ahsoka to negotiate with a very tiny picket line.
Key characters: Ahsoka, Huyang, assorted droids

2. Title: The Owl Courier Service
Hook: Morai starts delivering mysteriously important packages that only Ahsoka can accept.
Comedic twist: Every delivery is actually someone else’s mail, turning Ahsoka into an accidental interstellar mailman.
Key characters: Ahsoka, Morai, confused locals

3. Title: Lightsabers Anonymous
Hook: Ahsoka is asked to host a support group for lightsabers with “personality issues.”
Comedic twist: The sabers bond over awkward ignitions and start rating their owners’ dramatic flourishes.
Key characters: Ahsoka, Huyang, quirky sabers

4. Title: The Padawan Swap Program
Hook: Ahsoka agrees to a one-week padawan exchange program with another Jedi instructor.
Comedic twist: The swap padawan is actually a precocious astromech who refuses to answer to anything but beeps.
Key characters: Ahsoka, visiting instructor, astromech

5. Title: The Conscience of the Cantina
Hook: Ahsoka takes a low-profile job as a cantina bouncer to avoid galactic politics.
Comedic twist: She keeps solving everyone’s problems instead of throwing anyone out, turning the cantina into therapy night.
Key characters: Ahsoka, bartender, regulars

6. Title: The Great Map Bake-Off
Hook: Ahsoka enters a galaxy-wide cooking contest to win a star map as the grand prize.
Comedic twist: She keeps using the Force to levitate soufflés, and the judges can’t decide if that’s cheating or genius.
Key characters: Ahsoka, judges, rival chefs
