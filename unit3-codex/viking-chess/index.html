Rules (Compact)

  • Pieces move any number of squares orthogonally. No jumping.
  • Capture by sandwiching an enemy between two of your pieces.
  • Attackers win by surrounding the king on all four sides.
  • Defenders win if the king reaches a corner square.
  • Only the king may enter the corner squares.
